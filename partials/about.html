MY STORY

I fell in love with programming back in December of 2014 when I began building mobile apps for corporate conferences. I would spend my nights and weekends reading and watching tutorials and eventually joined Code for Charlotte to pursue my hobby for programming. I loved (and still love) meeting people in tech and hearing about their career journey as programmers. Though I had no idea what I was doing, I was determined, passionate, and excited to soak up as much knowledge as I possibly could.
Quickly, my hobby began to seep into projects at work and in March of 2015, I began researching a way to take my skills to the next level. After considering going back for a masters in Computer Science and a few bootcamps, I made the decision to attend a 6-month, Full Stack Immersive program at Galvanize in Denver, Colorado.
Programming has given me a great deal of confidence in my ability to solve problems and learn new things. There is no greater reward than when you work on a problem for days and finally solve it, or when you learn a new component of a language or framework.
